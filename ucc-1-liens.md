# UCC-1 Liens and Residential Solar

A **UCC-1 lien** is a public filing that gives a lender or third party a secured interest in property.
In residential solar, UCC-1 filings are commonly used with **solar loans, leases, or third-party financing**.

## Why UCC-1 Liens Are a Problem for Homeowners

When a UCC-1 lien is filed in connection with a solar system, it can:

* Interfere with **mortgage underwriting**
* Delay or block **refinances**
* Complicate **home sales**
* Reduce **buyer confidence**
* Create confusion about what is owned versus financed

In many cases, buyers and lenders discover UCC-1 filings **late in the transaction**, creating closing risk.
